Why prints

We don't remember files. We remember objects.

Your guests will shoot a hundred photos tonight and never open them again. The one they can hold, they keep for years. A print is a memory with a body.

What it costs you

Nothing.

Your guests or a sponsor cover the prints. There are two ways to run it.

Per print

Guests pay for their own prints on the spot, the way they'd buy anything else at your event.

Sponsored

A sponsor covers a set number, so guests take them home free, with the sponsor's name on every print.
